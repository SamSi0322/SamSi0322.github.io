About

I am an undergraduate researcher at the University of Waterloo, ECE Department, working on AI-driven tools for programming education. My research focuses on using large language models and multimodal signals (video, execution traces, code) to automatically debug, tutor, and evaluate block-based programs. I have published 4 first-authored papers in this area, including one accepted at FSE 2026.
